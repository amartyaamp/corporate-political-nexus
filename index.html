## The corporate political nexus

Ever wondered whom the power stays in the world's largest democracy? How the so-called leaders of the country are related to the corporates ( bureaucracy) and vice-versa ?

This project is an endeavour to give answers to these questions. Consists of-
1. scrapy modules written to scrape relevant data
2. graph db models to store the information for study
3. web app in flask for disemmination of the data and its visualisations.
